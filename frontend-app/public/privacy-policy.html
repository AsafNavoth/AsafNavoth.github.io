Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data collection

Utanki and the Utanki AnkiConnect browser extension do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Utanki web app

The Utanki web app (utanki.app) lets you search for lyrics and create Anki flashcards. Search queries and lyrics are sent to our backend API to provide results. We do not store or log this data for analytics or marketing purposes.

Utanki AnkiConnect extension

The extension acts as a bridge between the Utanki web app and AnkiConnect running on your computer. Messages are passed directly between these two—nothing is stored or sent to any external servers. All communication stays on your device.

Permissions

The extension requires access to localhost:8765 to communicate with AnkiConnect. It only runs on utanki.app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Utanki website.
